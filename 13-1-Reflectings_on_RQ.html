Proposal Paper 3: REVISED Important Research Question


Assignment Prompt

  • What is the research question motivating your proposal and why is it important?

  • Importance Criteria:

    • Impact on the real world, AND

    • Impact on the academic literature

Your task in the third paper is to revise your first paper so that your importance argument does both of these things

  • The new element of importance requires you to explicitly connect your work to established scientific literature


Remember, the scientific research is our best estimate of current knowledge

  • e.g. what we know about why the world works the way it does


This means your question is important ONLY if it:

  1. Is connected to issues of importance in the real world,

  2. Builds on what has come before it, AND

  3. Proposes to push us beyond what we already know


In short, the importance argument must show that the topic is impactful in the political world AND that it fills a gap in the academic literature


The funny thing is that you can’t easily do this from the beginning of the project

  • You need a draft question to guide you to the relevant literature

  • Then you have to summarize, organize and analyze that literature BEFORE you can make the argument your RQ is important to science!


Your next job is to take your expertise back to the research question!

  • Is your original question something we don’t already have a good answer to?

  • How could we tweak your question to make it target the unknown more precisely?


Any questions on this new element of importance?
